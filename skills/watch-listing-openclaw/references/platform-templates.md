# Platform Templates — Copy-Paste Structures
## For All Vardalux Watch Listing Platforms

**Source of truth:** The Vardalux Way v1.3 (Sections 5, 7, 8, 9, 13)

**Voice standard:** Ogilvy-informed conversational authority. Thoughts connect and
build. Specificity over superlatives. No staccato, no tagline-as-headline, no
motivational-poster energy. Every sentence earns its place. Write like someone
explaining how they think about a watch, not performing expertise.

**Identity anchor:** Vardalux are dealers who transact with purpose. Not commodity
flippers. The thinking behind each deal is the differentiator.

## CONDITION LANGUAGE REFERENCE

**Scale (use ONLY these terms):**
- **Excellent:** Minimal to no wear, could pass as unworn to casual observer
- **Very Good:** Light wear consistent with careful ownership, fully functional
- **Good:** Visible wear, honest use, still presentable and functional

**Never use "Mint."** Overused and meaningless.

**Always disclose:**
- Specific wear locations (clasp, lugs, caseback, crystal)
- Box/papers condition if different from watch condition
- Service needs or recommendations
- Missing links, accessories, or documentation
- "Service history unknown but running accurately" when service history is not known

**Condition disclosure examples:**

Excellent: "Watch is in excellent condition with only minor surface marks on
[specific location], consistent with light wear. [Other components] show no
notable wear."

Very Good: "Watch shows honest wear consistent with regular use. [Specific areas]
have visible marks including [describe]. All functions operate correctly."

Good: "Watch has been worn regularly and shows corresponding patina and wear.
[Specific detailed description]. Fully functional, with character that reflects
its history."
